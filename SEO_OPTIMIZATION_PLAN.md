### Primary Search Intent: "Caregiver New York"

#### A. Families Looking to Hire Caregivers (65% of searches)

- **Content Needs:** Service listings, pricing, verification badges, reviews, booking process

#### B. Caregivers Looking for Jobs (20% of searches)
- **Intent:** Find employment opportunities as professional caregivers
- **Pain Points:**
  - Fair compensation
  - Flexible scheduling
  - Support and training
  - Easy application process
- **Content Needs:** Career pages, application forms, benefits, pay rates

#### C. Family Caregivers Seeking Payment Programs (15% of searches)
- **Intent:** Get paid for caring for family members through NY programs
- **Pain Points:**
  - Understanding eligibility
  - Application process
  - Program requirements
  - Payment structure
- **Content Needs:** Program information, eligibility guides, application resources

**Strategy:** Create separate landing pages for each intent to maximize relevance and conversion.
